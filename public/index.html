Vanishing Pictures for iMessage

Now you see it, now you don't. GhostPics is the only secure iMessage app to send vanishing pictures to other iOS 10 users. Send your surprise images with animations including flash, fade or blind effects! And rest easy knowing that they won’t be able to share the surprise with anyone else.

Using GhostPics is simple

        
  1. Select a picture with the Pick Photo button
    2.         
  2. Choose any effect you want to display it with.
    3.        
  3. Send! It's that simple. Your recipient will only be able to open and view the image in GhostPics, and only once. It vanishes immediately after viewing.

Frequently Asked Questions

A: Yes, for the moment GhostPics is iOS 10 only, to ensures the security of the photos you share.
